Who We Are

Built on Craft.
Bound by Honor.

The Order of the White Lotus is the most sought-after clan on MCVerse. A few months old, yet already the standard every other clan is measured against. We are builders, stewards, and leaders — people who take pride in their work and show up for one another.

Membership here is not a title. It is a commitment. To the community, to the craft, and to something larger than any single build or battle.

Craft

Every contribution matters — from grand clan builds to quietly restocking shared storage for a clanmate you've never met.

Community

No member should feel peripheral. We actively draw everyone in, especially those outside the main projects.

Continuity

Those who leave are welcomed back at the rank they earned. We hold no grudges — only rosters.

Leadership

The Grand Loti govern by quorum. No single voice rules. Every major decision requires consensus.

Hierarchy

The Rank Structure

Climb the main track, or earn your place through mastery of a specialist role. Every path is honourable.

Main Track — Disciple to Grand Lotus
Leadership
Grand Lotus
The governing council. Decisions require quorum of at least two. The highest honour any member can attain.
Senior
Crescent Lotus
Second highest. Trusted veterans who embody the Order's values and serve as pillars of the community.
Established
Lotus Sentry
Core members of the Order. Proven contributors recognised for consistent, meaningful service.
Entry
Lotus Disciple
New members finding their footing within the Order. The beginning of the journey.
Specialist Roles — Expertise Without Rank
Specialist
Lotus Quarterwarden
Stewards of clan resources, storage, and logistics. The backbone that keeps the Order running.
Specialist
Lotus Sensei
Guides and mentors. They welcome new disciples and pass down the knowledge of the Order.
Specialist
Lotus Scholar
Keepers of lore, history, and strategy. Knowledge is their weapon and their gift to the clan.

Any member may hold a specialist role. They exist alongside, not above, the main track.

Governance

How We Run

Clear, fair rules that protect the community and reward dedication.

01
Inactivity Policy

Up to 30 days of unannounced inactivity is permitted at any rank. After that, council reaches out. Announced absences of any length are always fine — life happens, and we respect that.

02
Council Quorum

Major decisions require at minimum 2 Grand Loti and 2 other members. A fifth is added when possible to break ties. Discord polls serve when in-person quorum cannot be met.

03
Open Return Policy

Anyone removed for inactivity is welcome back when they return — likely restored to the rank they left. We carry no grudges. The door is always open.

04
Contribution Points

A clan point system is in development. Building, farming, stocking storage, landscaping, and unique contributions all earn recognition. Top contributors are honoured monthly with prizes and clout.

05
Selective Membership

We receive 3–5 join requests every single week. Every applicant is vetted with clan input. Exclusivity is not arrogance — it is what makes belonging here mean something.

06
Path to Grand Lotus

Any member may become Grand Lotus. No rigid formula — contribute meaningfully, represent the clan well across the server, take ownership, and lead. The rest follows naturally.

Membership

Ready to Seek Entry?

The Order does not recruit. If you believe you belong here, make yourself known on the MCVerse forums or reach out to a Grand Lotus directly.

Who We Are

Built on Excellence,
Rooted in Community

The Order of the White Lotus is one of the most sought-after clans on MCVerse. We are builders, leaders, and contributors — people who take pride in their craft and show up for one another.

Being in this clan isn't just about impressive builds. It's about community, shared resources, and making the server better for everyone around us. New members are carefully vetted to ensure we remain a top-tier fellowship.

Craft

We take pride in meaningful builds and contributions — from grand clan projects to stocking shared storage for a clanmate.

Community

No member should feel like an outsider. We actively include everyone, especially those less involved in major projects.

Continuity

Founding members, long-timers, and newcomers alike are valued. Those who leave and return are welcomed back at their earned rank.

Leadership

Grand Loti govern by quorum — two or more must agree on major decisions. No single voice rules the Order.

Governance

Clan Policy

Clear, fair rules that protect the community and reward dedication.

🗓
Inactivity Policy

Members receive up to 30 days of unannounced inactivity. After that, the council reaches out via Discord. No response triggers a vote. Announced absences of any length are always fine — life happens.

⚖️
Council Quorum

Major decisions require a minimum of 2 Grand Loti and 2 other members. A 5th member joins when possible to avoid split votes. Discord polls are used when in-person quorum isn't achievable.

↩️
Open Return Policy

Anyone removed for inactivity is always welcome to return. On rejoining, members are likely restored to the rank they held before leaving. We carry no grudges — only rosters.

⭐
Contribution Points

A points system is in development to recognize contributors: stocking shared storage, building, creating farms, landscaping, and unique contributions all earn recognition. Top contributors are rewarded monthly.

🚪
Selective Membership

We receive 3–5 join requests every week and remain deliberate about who joins. All new members are vetted with clan input. Exclusivity is a feature, not a flaw — it's what makes belonging here meaningful.

🌸
Path to Grand Lotus

Any member can become Grand Lotus. There's no rigid formula — contribute meaningfully, represent the clan well in the broader server, take ownership of projects, and show up as a leader.
