Who We Are

Built on Excellence,
Rooted in Community

The Order of the White Lotus is one of the most sought-after clans on MCVerse. We are builders, leaders, and contributors — people who take pride in their craft and show up for one another.

Being in this clan isn't just about impressive builds. It's about community, shared resources, and making the server better for everyone around us. New members are carefully vetted to ensure we remain a top-tier fellowship.

Craft

We take pride in meaningful builds and contributions — from grand clan projects to stocking shared storage for a clanmate.

Community

No member should feel like an outsider. We actively include everyone, especially those less involved in major projects.

Continuity

Founding members, long-timers, and newcomers alike are valued. Those who leave and return are welcomed back at their earned rank.

Leadership

Grand Loti govern by quorum — two or more must agree on major decisions. No single voice rules the Order.

Governance

Clan Policy

Clear, fair rules that protect the community and reward dedication.

🗓
Inactivity Policy

Members receive up to 30 days of unannounced inactivity. After that, the council reaches out via Discord. No response triggers a vote. Announced absences of any length are always fine — life happens.

⚖️
Council Quorum

Major decisions require a minimum of 2 Grand Loti and 2 other members. A 5th member joins when possible to avoid split votes. Discord polls are used when in-person quorum isn't achievable.

↩️
Open Return Policy

Anyone removed for inactivity is always welcome to return. On rejoining, members are likely restored to the rank they held before leaving. We carry no grudges — only rosters.

⭐
Contribution Points

A points system is in development to recognize contributors: stocking shared storage, building, creating farms, landscaping, and unique contributions all earn recognition. Top contributors are rewarded monthly.

🚪
Selective Membership

We receive 3–5 join requests every week and remain deliberate about who joins. All new members are vetted with clan input. Exclusivity is a feature, not a flaw — it's what makes belonging here meaningful.

🌸
Path to Grand Lotus

Any member can become Grand Lotus. There's no rigid formula — contribute meaningfully, represent the clan well in the broader server, take ownership of projects, and show up as a leader.

Membership

Ready to Seek Entry?

The Order does not recruit — we select. If you believe you have what it takes, make yourself known on the MCVerse forums or reach out to a Grand Lotus directly.
